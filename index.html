Chefchaouen

Introduction:

Chefchaouen is a city located in northern Morocco that was founded

in 1471 by Moulay Ali ibn Rashid al Alami. Hidden between the Rif

mountains.

The Hisyory of Chefchaouen:

Chefchaouen began as a Kasbah for the purpose of defending against

Portuguese invasions, it later became the refuge for Moors and Jews

who were expelled from Spain.

The Origins of Chefchaouen's blue color:

There are many theories regarding Chefchaouen's blue color:

  • The blue color represents the sky and heaven.
  • Introduction by Jewish refugees as a symbol of peace and safety.
  • The blue color scares away mosqitoes.

Regardless of its origins, the blue color creates a stunning atmosphere

that attracts visitors from all over the world.

Conclusion:

Chefchaouen is a magnificent city that is truly worth the visit, it provides

an unforgettable experience for every tourist, from the blue buildings to the

nice people, it captures the heart of every visitor.
